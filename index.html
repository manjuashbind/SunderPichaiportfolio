About Me

An immigrant American, working as head of the Google- top search engine company of the world. This enough is a good introduction of me! I have worked hard to steer my way to the top. I am dedicated, talented and skilled to recognize my strengths and work on them to make myself stronger.

CAREER JOURNEY


I worked in engineering and product management at Applied Materials and in management consulting at McKinsey & Company. I joined Google in 2004, where I led the product management and innovation efforts for a suite of Google's client software products, including Google Chrome and ChromeOS, as well as being largely responsible for Google Drive. I went on to oversee the development of other applications such as Gmail and Google Maps.


On November 19, 2009,I gave a demonstration of ChromeOS; the Chromebook was released for trial and testing in 2011, and released to the public in 2012.On May 20, 2010, announced the open-sourcing of the new video codec VP8 by Google and introduced the new video format, WebM.


On March 13, 2013, we added Android to the list of Google products that he oversaw. Android was formerly managed by Andy Rubin. He was a director of Jive Software from April 2011 to July 30, 2013.I was selected to become the next CEO of Google on August 10, 2015 after previously being appointed Product Chief by CEO, Larry Page.

On October 24, 2015, I stepped into the new position at the completion of the formation of Alphabet Inc., the new holding company for the Google company family.

ACHIEVEMENTS

I am the CEO of Google and Alphabet and serves on Alphabet's Board of Directors. Under my leadership, Google has been focused on developing products and services, powered by the latest advances in AI, that offer help in moments big and small.

In 2022, I was awarded the Padma Bhushan in the category of Trade and Industry from the Government of India, the country's third-highest civilian award.India’s Ambassador to the US Taranjit Singh Sandhu handed over the award in San Francisco .I am deeply grateful to the Indian government and the people of India for this immense honour. It is incredibly meaningful to be honoured in this way by the country that shaped me
